STAR Scholarship Program

STAR hosts a scholarship program partnered with Mechanics Bank for low-income women of color from Bay Area schools, totaling $10,000 a year split among five recipients. The program is focused on helping our recipients attend their dream schools, by filling the gap between institutional financial aid and the actual cost of living. Please reach out to our outreach lead if you are interested! Our application releases in the Fall.

School Outreach Events

STAR hosts outreach events with elementary, middle, and high schools both on and off campus. Last semester, we ran events for Alameda and Palo Alto High School. We focus on careers and education and would love to work with you! Reach out to our outreach lead if interested!
